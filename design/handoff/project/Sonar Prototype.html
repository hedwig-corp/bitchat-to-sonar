Sonar — interactive prototype

v2 · dark-first · radar discovery · transport-colored chats

What changed from bitchat v1

  • Rebranded to Sonar — cyan on cool neutrals, dark-first.
  • Radar discovery: people placed by Bluetooth proximity, sweep + pulse, list one tap away. Out-of-range friends appear as faded edge ghosts.
  • iMessage-style transport colors: your bubbles are cyan when delivered nearby over Bluetooth, indigo when they travel over the internet (Nostr). Routing is automatic.
  • XChat-inspired: bigger round avatars, unread dot, floating search + discover bar, Settings with profile card + grouped sections.

Try

  • Tap the compose/rings button (or top-right) → the radar; toggle Radar ↔ List.
  • DM Maya (in range → cyan) vs Sofia (out of range → indigo).
  • Tap the status chip to go Offline, then message Sofia — it queues: \u201cWaiting to send\u201d.
  • Your avatar (top-left) → Settings: identity, network, emergency wipe.
  • Triple-tap the sonar title for emergency wipe.
  • / in any composer still reveals the command layer.
  • Bitcoin: claim Maya’s sealed coin; “+” in a DM → Send bitcoin (keypad); radar → tap a peer → “Send sats”. Gold = money; rails follow the transport (ecash over Bluetooth / Lightning).
