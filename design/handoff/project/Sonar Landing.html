Why Sonar

A messenger that treats the network as a feature.

Works without internet

Bluetooth mesh relays messages phone-to-phone. Festivals, flights, blackouts, protests — if people are around, Sonar works.

Reaches anyone, anywhere

Out of range? Messages travel over Nostr — an open relay network nobody owns. No company server sits between you and your friends.

Private by design

Direct messages are end-to-end encrypted, always. Your identity is a key created on your phone — verify friends in person with a safety number.

Pay a friend

Send money as easily as a message.

Settle a dinner across the table or send cash across the world. The payment takes the same path your message would — phone-to-phone over Bluetooth when they’re near, over the internet when they’re not. It arrives sealed; they tap to open.

Bitcoin mode is off by default. You see amounts in your own currency. Flip it on for sats, Lightning and ecash — your call.

Location channels

Places, not coordinates.

Wherever you stand, Sonar shows one “Around you” place — zoom from your block to the whole region with a single tap. Real place names, never raw coordinates, and never an endless list of empty rooms.
